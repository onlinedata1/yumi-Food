Ajza 

.Adha cup khana ka tail.
.5 chamach zaitoon ka tail.
.2 chamach kaali mirch.
.2 chamach zeera .
.3 chamach adraak , lehsan aur haari mirch ki paste .
.2 chamach laal mirch.
.1 chamach haldee (optional).
.1 chamach namak hasab-e-zaiqa .
.500 grams safaid chaana.
.2 chamach dhania for toping.
.3 adad haari mirch for toping.

Tarqeeb

.chaano ko raat bhar paani me bhigona ka baad usa hasab-e-zaiqa
 		 namak daal k ubaal layn.
.phir 3 cup paani daal k usko achi taara pees k paste bna layn
.ab adha cup khana bnana wala teel paan ya dejki ma daal k garam kr lyn. 
.phir adraak , lehsan aur haari mirch ki paste dal kr achi taara sa bhoon layn 
.ab laal mirch , kaali mirch , haldee daal kr bhoonayn . 
.phir is ma chaana ki paste daal kr haal kr lyn .
.akhir ma zeera , salt hasab-e-zaiqa , zaitoon ka tail 3 chamach daalo .
.ab hummus tyaar ho chuka h ab chulha band kr k zaitoon ka tail 2 chamach ,
dhania , 3 adat haari mirch kaati hui daal lo .
